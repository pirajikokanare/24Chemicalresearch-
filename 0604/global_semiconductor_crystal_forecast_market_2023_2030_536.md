</p><p>The Iridium Manganese Alloy Target market refers to the industry involved in the production, development, and sales of alloy targets made primarily from iridium and manganese. These alloy targets are essential materials used in various industrial applications, such as semiconductor manufacturing, thin-film deposition, and surface coating. Iridium is prized for its high melting point and corrosion resistance, while manganese enhances strength and oxidation resistance. The alloy’s versatility in electronics, automotive, and other high-performance industries drives the demand for these targets.</p><p>
