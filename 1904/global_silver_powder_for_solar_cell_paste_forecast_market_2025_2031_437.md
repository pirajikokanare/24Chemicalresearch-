</p><p><strong>Silver Powder for Solar Cell Paste</strong> refers to finely divided silver particles used in the conductive pastes essential for photovoltaic (PV) cell manufacturing. These pastes are screen-printed onto solar wafers to form the front and rear electrodes of the solar cells. Silver powder’s high conductivity, stability under elevated temperatures, and compatibility with various cell structures make it indispensable in the solar photovoltaic industry.</p><p>
</p><p>The primary function of silver powder is to enable efficient electron collection and transport, thus enhancing the overall efficiency and power output of solar cells. It is especially critical in advanced solar technologies such as PERC (Passivated Emitter Rear Cell), BSF (Back Surface Field), and N-type monocrystalline cells like N-PERT and TOPCon. Front silver paste, the dominant application segment, contains silver powder optimized for adhesion, conductivity, and firing through anti-reflective layers.</p><p>
