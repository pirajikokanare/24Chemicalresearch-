<h1>Solar Control Window Films Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Solar Control Window Films are specialized coatings applied to glass surfaces to regulate heat, glare, and UV radiation. These films improve energy efficiency, enhance privacy, and protect interior furnishings from fading. They are widely used in residential, commercial, and automotive applications to maintain comfortable indoor environments and reduce energy costs. Available in various types, including dyed, vacuum-coated, and multilayer films, these films are integral to modern building and vehicle design.</p><p>
