It is classified as a water-soluble dye and is known for its stability under light, heat, and acidic conditions. Beyond food, Indigotine finds application in the textile, plastic, and fiber industries due to its vibrant coloration and consistent quality. As regulatory approvals and consumer preference for visual appeal in consumables grow, the demand for this coloring agent is expected to continue its upward trajectory.</p><p>
