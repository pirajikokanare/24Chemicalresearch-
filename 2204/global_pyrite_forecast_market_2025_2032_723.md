<h1>Pyrite Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Pyrite, often referred to as "fool's gold" due to its golden-yellow metallic sheen, is one of the most prevalent sulfide minerals found in various geological settings. It is primarily composed of iron disulfide (FeS2) and is widely distributed in igneous, metamorphic, and sedimentary rocks. Pyrite forms at both high and low temperatures, which makes it a versatile mineral in terms of its geological occurrence. Beyond its aesthetic appeal, pyrite holds significant industrial value as a source of sulfur and sulfuric acid, contributing to the global chemical industry.</p><p>

The market’s main products are derived from large chalcopyrite and sphalerite deposits, where pyrite is commonly found as a secondary mineral.
