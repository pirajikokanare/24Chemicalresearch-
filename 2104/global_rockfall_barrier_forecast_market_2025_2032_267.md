<h1>Rockfall Barrier Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Rockfall barriers are engineered safety structures designed to prevent or mitigate the impact of falling rocks and debris from mountainous or unstable terrains onto roads, railways, and inhabited areas.
