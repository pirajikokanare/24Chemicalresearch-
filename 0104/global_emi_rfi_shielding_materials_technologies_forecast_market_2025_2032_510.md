<h1>EMI and RFI Shielding Materials and Technologies Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Electromagnetic interference (EMI) and radio-frequency interference (RFI) shielding materials and technologies are crucial in protecting electronic devices from unwanted electromagnetic waves and radio-frequency signals. These materials are used in various applications across industries to prevent the disruption of normal operation, ensuring that devices function effectively without external interference. The primary forms of EMI and RFI shielding materials include tapes, absorbers, foams, mesh, vent panels, and board-level shields. Each material plays a vital role in protecting sensitive components, especially in sectors where precision and safety are critical, such as aerospace, military, automotive, and medical industries.</p><p>
</p><p>In simpler terms, EMI and RFI shielding ensures the integrity of signals and systems by blocking or reducing the electromagnetic waves that can interfere with the device's normal functioning.
