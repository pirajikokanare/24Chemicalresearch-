<strong>Definition</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Heat-resistant ABS (HH ABS) is a grade of ABS known for its superior heat resistance, shock resistance, and outstanding workability. It is widely used in automotive parts and electrical devices due to its strength and durability. The vicat softening temperature of high heat-resistant ABS is 109°C, while that of super high heat-resistant ABS is 115°C.</p><p>
