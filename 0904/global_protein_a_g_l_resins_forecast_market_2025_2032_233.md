<h1>Protein A, G and L Resins Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Protein A, G, and L resins are high-performance affinity chromatography media used for antibody purification. These resins bind specifically to immunoglobulins (Ig), facilitating their isolation, immobilization, or detection. Each type—Protein A, Protein G, and Protein L—interacts with different regions of antibodies, making them useful in various applications across multiple species and antibody subclasses. These resins play a crucial role in biopharmaceutical manufacturing, diagnostics, and research laboratories.</p><p>
