<h1>Povidone-iodine for Agriculture Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Povidone-iodine is a chemical complex consisting of povidone and iodine, widely used in agriculture as a biocide and antiseptic agent. It is primarily utilized for its strong antimicrobial properties that help control and prevent plant diseases caused by bacteria, fungi, and viruses. The iodine component acts as an effective disinfectant, while povidone ensures an even distribution of iodine, offering comprehensive protection for crops. This chemical is commonly applied as a foliar spray, seed treatment, and for disinfecting agricultural equipment.</p><p>
