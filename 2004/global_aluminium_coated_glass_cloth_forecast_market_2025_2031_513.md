</p><p>Aluminium Coated Glass Cloth is a composite material made by bonding aluminum foil onto a fiberglass base. The result is a flexible and durable fabric with a smooth, reflective surface and superior thermal insulation properties. This cloth is designed to resist high temperatures, prevent moisture ingress, and offer excellent chemical resistance, making it ideal for industrial, construction, and transportation applications.</p><p>
