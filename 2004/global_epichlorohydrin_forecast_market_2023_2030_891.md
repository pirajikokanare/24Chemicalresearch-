<h1>Epichlorohydrin (ECH) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Epichlorohydrin (ECH) is an essential chemical compound primarily used in the production of epoxy resins. It is an organochlorine compound with the molecular formula C3H5ClO. ECH is a colorless, volatile liquid with a pungent odor and is highly reactive due to its epoxide and chloro functional groups. It serves as a key intermediate in manufacturing synthetic glycerin, water treatment chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and other industrial products.</p><p>
