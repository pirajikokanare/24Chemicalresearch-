<h1>Latin America Di-Ammonium Phosphate Market Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Di-Ammonium Phosphate (DAP) is a widely used phosphate fertilizer composed of two primary nutrients: nitrogen and phosphorus. It plays a crucial role in enhancing agricultural productivity by promoting plant root development, improving crop yield, and supporting soil fertility. The chemical formula for DAP is (NH4)2HPO4, making it a highly soluble and efficient nutrient source. Beyond agriculture, DAP is also utilized in various industrial applications, including fire retardants, brewing, and metal finishing.</p><p>
