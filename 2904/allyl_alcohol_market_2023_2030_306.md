<h1>Allyl Alcohol Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>
</p><p>Allyl alcohol is a toxic, water-soluble, and colorless liquid that plays a crucial role in several industrial applications. It is primarily used in the production of chemicals like butanediol (BDO), resins, and coupling agents. With a chemical formula of C3H6O, allyl alcohol is characterized by the presence of an -OH (hydroxyl) group attached to an allyl group (-CH2-CH=CH2), making it a valuable intermediate in the chemical industry. Allyl alcohol is produced through multiple processes, with the two primary methods being allyl acetate hydrolysis and propylene oxide (PO) isomerization. These production methods contribute to the significant demand for allyl alcohol, especially in sectors such as polymers, plastics, and other specialty chemicals.</p><p>
