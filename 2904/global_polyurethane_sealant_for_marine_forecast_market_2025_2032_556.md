<h1>Polyurethane Sealant for Marine Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Polyurethane (PU) sealants for marine applications are specialized adhesives designed to provide durable, flexible, and water-resistant sealing solutions for boats, ships, and other marine vessels. These sealants offer excellent adhesion to a variety of surfaces, including metal, fiberglass, and wood, making them ideal for sealing joints, decks, and hulls.
