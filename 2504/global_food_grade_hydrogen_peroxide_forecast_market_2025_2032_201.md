<strong>Definition</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Food-grade hydrogen peroxide is a diluted form of hydrogen peroxide that contains a concentration of 35% or less. It is specifically processed to be safe for use in food-related applications. This chemical compound is utilized in the food industry for various purposes, including as a disinfectant, bleaching agent, and preservative. In food processing, hydrogen peroxide is primarily used for sterilizing equipment, washing fruits and vegetables, and disinfecting food packaging materials. It is also employed in food whitening, removing contaminants, and purifying water used in food processing.</p><p>
</p><p>The food-grade hydrogen peroxide market encompasses the production, distribution, and use of this chemical across multiple industries, including food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, and water treatment.
