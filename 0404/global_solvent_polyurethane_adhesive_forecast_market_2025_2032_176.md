<h1>Solvent Polyurethane Adhesive Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>
</p><p>Solvent polyurethane adhesive is a formulation containing polyurethane polymers dissolved in solvents, providing superior bonding capabilities. These adhesives are widely used in industries such as packaging, automotive, footwear, and more due to their high strength, flexibility, and durability. Their versatility makes them suitable for applications requiring resistance to environmental factors such as moisture, temperature variations, and chemical exposure.</p><p>
