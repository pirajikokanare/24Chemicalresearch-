</p><p>Nano Calcium Carbonate (nano CaCO3) refers to ultra-fine calcium carbonate particles with a particle size typically less than 100 nanometers. This material is known for its high purity, controlled particle size, surface area, and superior dispersion properties. Derived from natural limestone sources or synthesized through chemical processes, nano calcium carbonate is widely used as a functional additive in various industries, including plastics, rubber, paints and coatings, adhesives, and paper manufacturing.</p><p>
</p><p>The unique characteristics of nano calcium carbonate, such as its nano-scale size and enhanced surface reactivity, make it a valuable ingredient for improving mechanical properties, enhancing surface gloss, increasing impact resistance, and boosting thermal stability in end-use products. Additionally, its eco-friendly nature and non-toxic profile have further boosted its adoption in pharmaceuticals, food packaging, and personal care products.</p><p>
