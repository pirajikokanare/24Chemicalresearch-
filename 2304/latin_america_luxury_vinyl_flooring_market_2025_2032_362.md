</p><p><strong>Opportunities</strong>:</p><p>

	</p><p><strong>Growth in the Commercial Sector</strong>: The growing commercial real estate sector in Latin America presents a significant opportunity for LVT manufacturers. </p><p><strong>Challenges</strong>:</p><p>

	</p><p><strong>Fluctuating Raw Material Prices</strong>: The cost of raw materials, particularly PVC, can be volatile, which may affect manufacturers' ability to offer competitive prices, especially during periods of economic instability.</p><p><strong>Market Fragmentation</strong>: The Latin American LVT market is highly fragmented, with numerous small and large players competing for market share. This intensifies competition and makes it challenging for manufacturers to differentiate themselves solely based on price.</p><p>
