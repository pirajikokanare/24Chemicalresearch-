</p><p>Flotation depressants are chemical agents used in the froth flotation process to prevent certain minerals from floating while allowing the target mineral to be separated efficiently. These depressants play a crucial role in mining and mineral processing industries by ensuring selective separation, enhancing flotation efficiency, and improving the quality of recovered minerals.</p><p>
</p><p>Common flotation depressants include sodium cyanide, sodium sulfide, starch, dextrin, and organic compounds, each having unique applications in different mineral processing industries.</p><p>
