<h1>Phyto Squalane Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Phyto Squalane is a high-performance emollient derived from plant-based sources such as olives and sugarcane. It is widely used in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and other applications due to its excellent moisturizing properties, lightweight texture, and ability to mimic the skin’s natural sebum. Unlike animal-derived squalane, phyto squalane is considered a sustainable and cruelty-free alternative, making it a preferred choice for various industries.</p><p>
