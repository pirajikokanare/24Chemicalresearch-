<h1>Recycled Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Recycled Polyethylene Terephthalate (rPET) refers to the reprocessed form of polyethylene terephthalate (PET), a widely used thermoplastic polymer. PET is primarily derived from terephthalic acid and ethylene glycol, forming a durable and versatile material. Recycling PET helps in reducing plastic waste and contributes to sustainability by minimizing environmental pollution. rPET is commonly used in applications such as textile fiber production, food and beverage containers, sheet manufacturing, and industrial strapping.</p><p>
