<h1>Water Based Lithography Inks Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Water-based lithography inks are specially formulated inks that utilize water as a primary solvent, making them an environmentally friendly alternative to solvent-based inks. These inks are primarily used in the printing industry for newspapers, packaging, magazines, and promotional materials. They are known for their excellent adhesion, fast drying times, and low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions, making them ideal for sustainable printing solutions.</p><p>
