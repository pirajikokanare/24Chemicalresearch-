They demonstrate minimal irreversible losses in magnetic properties over time, ensuring their performance and reliability over extended periods. 6. Niche applications: SmCo magnets find extensive use in niche applications that require specific magnetic properties. These include aerospace and defense applications, high-temperature environments, magnetic separators, magnetic couplings, and high-end audio equipment, among others. 7. Limited availability: SmCo magnets are considered rare-earth magnets, and their production relies on the availability of raw materials, such as samarium and cobalt.
