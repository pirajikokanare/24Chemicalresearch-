<h1>Soda-Lime Glass Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Soda-lime glass, also known as soda-lime-silica glass, is the most common type of glass produced globally. It is primarily composed of silica (SiO2), soda (Na2O), and lime (CaO). This glass type is widely used due to its affordability, ease of production, and versatility across multiple industries. Its primary applications include packaging (bottles and jars), construction (windows and facades), and automotive components. The widespread availability of raw materials and its ability to be easily shaped and recycled make soda-lime glass a preferred choice in various sectors.</p><p>
