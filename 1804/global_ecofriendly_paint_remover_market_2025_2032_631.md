<strong>Drivers</strong></p><p>

	</p><p><strong>Environmental Awareness</strong>: The primary driver for the eco-friendly paint remover market is the increasing environmental consciousness of both consumers and businesses. This can pose challenges to widespread adoption.</p><p><strong>Perceived Effectiveness</strong>: Some consumers may perceive eco-friendly paint removers as less effective compared to traditional chemical-based options, especially for heavy-duty applications.
