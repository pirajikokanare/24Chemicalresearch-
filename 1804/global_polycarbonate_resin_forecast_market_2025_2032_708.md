<h1>Polycarbonate (PC) Resin Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Polycarbonates (PC) are a group of thermoplastic polymers characterized by the presence of carbonate groups in their chemical structure. Known for their high strength, toughness, and optical clarity, polycarbonates are versatile materials used across a wide range of industries. Some specific grades of polycarbonates offer transparency, making them ideal for applications in various fields like automotive, electronics, and construction.</p><p>
</p><p>These polymers are not only easily molded and thermoformed, but also exhibit excellent resistance to impact and heat, making them suitable for high-performance applications.
