</p><p>Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) membranes are synthetic polymeric materials primarily used for waterproofing and separation applications in industries such as construction, wastewater treatment, roofing, and transportation infrastructure. Composed mainly of PVC resins, plasticizers, stabilizers, and other additives, these membranes exhibit high flexibility, durability, and chemical resistance. Their impermeable nature and long service life make them highly effective for containment and structural protection against moisture and environmental degradation. These membranes are generally used in single-ply systems or as part of composite barrier systems.</p><p>
