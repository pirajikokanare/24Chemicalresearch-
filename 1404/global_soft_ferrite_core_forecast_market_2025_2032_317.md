<h1>Soft Ferrite Core Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Soft ferrite cores are magnetic components used in a variety of electronic applications, including power transformers, inductors, and electromagnetic interference (EMI) suppression. These materials exhibit high electrical resistance and low eddy current losses, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. They are composed primarily of iron oxide combined with other metal oxides, such as manganese and zinc, to enhance their magnetic properties.</p><p>
