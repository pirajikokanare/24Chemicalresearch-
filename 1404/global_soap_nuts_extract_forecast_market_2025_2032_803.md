</p><p><strong>Overview</strong></p><p>
</p><p><strong>Soap nut extract</strong> is derived from the dried fruit of plants belonging to the Sapindus genus, which are native to tropical and subtropical regions across the world. These nuts contain natural saponins, which act as surfactants, making them a popular eco-friendly alternative to synthetic detergents and cleaning agents. Soap nuts extract is widely used in industries such as personal care, household cleaning, and pharmaceuticals due to its hypoallergenic and biodegradable properties.</p><p>
</p><p>The extract is available in various forms, including liquid concentrates, powders, and dried raw nuts, catering to diverse consumer needs. With rising environmental consciousness and demand for organic products, soap nuts extract has gained significant traction in both developed and developing markets.</p><p>
