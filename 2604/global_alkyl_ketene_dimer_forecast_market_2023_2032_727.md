<h1>Alkyl Ketene Dimer (AKD) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Alkyl Ketene Dimer (AKD) is an organic compound widely used as a sizing agent in the paper industry to enhance water resistance and printability. Structurally, AKD is based on the oxetan-2-one four-membered ring system, similar to propiolactone and diketene. It reacts with cellulose fibers in paper, forming a hydrophobic layer that prevents ink spreading and enhances durability.</p><p>
