<strong>Definition</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Cast Nylon, also known as polyamide, is a thermoplastic polymer that offers excellent mechanical strength, wear resistance, and low friction properties. It is widely used in industries such as automotive, construction, aerospace, and machinery for applications including gears, bearings, rollers, and bushings. Cast Nylon is produced through the polymerization of caprolactam and offers superior performance in high-load and high-temperature environments.</p><p>
