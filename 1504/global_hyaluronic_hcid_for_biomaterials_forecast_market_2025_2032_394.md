It plays a critical role in tissue hydration, cellular function, and wound healing. Owing to its biocompatibility, viscoelasticity, and moisture-retention capabilities, HA is extensively utilized in <strong>ophthalmology</strong>, <strong>orthopedics</strong>, <strong>cosmetic dermatology</strong>, and <strong>tissue engineering</strong>. Its widespread adoption is bolstered by the increasing prevalence of age-related diseases and aesthetic enhancements, which continues to stimulate its integration into medical and cosmetic biomaterials globally.</p><p>
