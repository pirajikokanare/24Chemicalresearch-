<h1>Hail Protection Net Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Hail protection net is a type of protective covering used in agriculture to prevent damage to crops from hailstorms. It is typically made of high-density polyethylene (HDPE) or polypropylene (PP) material that is woven into a net-like structure with varying mesh sizes. The netting can be fixed onto structures such as poles, wires, or frames above the crops to provide a physical barrier between the hailstones and the plants.</p><p>
