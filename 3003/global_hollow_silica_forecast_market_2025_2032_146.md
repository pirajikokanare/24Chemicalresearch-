<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Hollow silica, a type of porous material, is characterized by its unique internal cavity structure, high surface area, and excellent adsorption capabilities. Due to its remarkable permeability and controlled material transfer, it finds extensive applications in drug delivery, chromatography, environmental protection, and catalysis. </p><p>
</p><p>The hollow structure enables it to store, load, and release guest molecules such as drugs, flavors, dyes, and bacteriocins, making it highly valuable across various industries.</p><p>
