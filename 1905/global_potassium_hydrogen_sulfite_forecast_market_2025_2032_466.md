<h1>Potassium Hydrogen Sulfite Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Potassium bisulfite, commonly known as potassium hydrogen sulfite, is a chemical compound with a strong sulfurous odor. It is primarily used as a food preservative, antioxidant, and sterilizing agent. The compound reacts with acids present in food, releasing sulfur dioxide (SO2), which effectively inhibits the growth of harmful microorganisms. Due to its versatile applications, potassium hydrogen sulfite is extensively used in the food &amp; beverage industry, pharmaceuticals, chemicals, wastewater treatment, and photography.</p><p>
